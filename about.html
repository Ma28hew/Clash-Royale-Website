Main Strategies

The main strategy in fireball bait is to use weak troops to bait your opponent into using their big spell (Lightning, Poison, Rocket or Fireball). Then you rush with your Royale Hogs; now that your opponent does not have a big spell to stop them.


Split Lane Push

A split lane push is when you are threatening both of your opponents princess towers at the same time. Fire ball bait is realy good at this because it has multiple cards that can split their troops into different directions (Royal Hogs, Zappies and Royal Recruits).
